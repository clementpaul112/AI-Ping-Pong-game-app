Instructions

  1. Keep your laptop screen straight.
  2. Move yourself aproximetly 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending or decending order, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.
  4. Now press the play button,and again move yourself aproximetly 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
  5. The red paddel is your paddel that will move as per the movement of your right wrist.
